Dino Games

  • Prep for chronological games reel!

dungeon crawler

[b-reel: runner gifs]

  • i was going for something basic, and that’s what I got
    • not much to say here beyond what you’re seeing
    • arrows, coins, keys, doors, a broken boss battle

runner

[b-reel: runner gifs]

  • runner gave me a chance to try to add platforms dynamically
    • the player needs to ’complete’ the room
    • it’ll keep showing up until they get the last leaf or coin
  • there are several later ideas built on this
    • the woods
    • dream runner
    • leaf runner

ghost house

[b-reel: ghost house gifs]

  • godot wild jam (dec 2022)
  • in ghost house, you wander a spooky set of rooms
    • stun goombas with your flashlight then kick ’em over
  • that’s it, no win state, enemies regen when you use doors

harvey

[b-reel: harvey gifs]

  • ludum dare winter (jam 2023)
  • an overcooked-like but for farming
    • you get seeds, plant them, water them, then deliver the produce
  • a nice win: subclassed the player and created bots so you don’t have to play alone

gunner

[b-reel: gunner gifs]

  • basically break the targets with a bow-and-arrow
    • yeah, name doesn’t quite fit
  • mostly i worked on adding ’juicy’ features while whatching a string of juice-based youtube videos
    • check out the full playlist

[link juicy videos playlist]

tower jet

[b-reel: tower jet gifs]

  • godot wild jam (jan 2023)
  • built on top of gunner, intended as a going-up ’Down Well’
    • got lost in some procedural level generation, which taught me alot but ultimately did not survive the godot 3 to 4 transition
    • i’ve since built up a different proc-gen style, don’t worry

juicy snake

[b-reel: juicy snake]

  • juicy jam 2 (jan 2023)
  • intended as just snake, but really juicy
  • this one was completely different, and ended up being really buggy
  • I’d like to take another shot at this some day

hatbot

[b-reel: hatbot]

  • probably the closest thing to a ’real’ game I’ve made
  • metroidvania jam 19 (feb-march 23)
  • built an addon called Hotel for tracking entity health, player items across ’zones’
    • i’ve since abandoned this addon completely
  • 5 zones, 2 bosses (3 boss battles), 3 powerups, some enemies, 1 easter egg
  • not too shabby!

herd

[b-reel: herd]

  • bullet hell jam (may 23, did not submit)
  • i liked the idea of calling sheep (pikman style)
    • then ’herding’ them in to some safe zones
  • i never got around to filling in those flashing square placeholders…

super elevator level

[b-reel: super elevator level]

  • go godot jam (may 23)
  • built out a beat em up controller in ’beehive’ (a dino addon)
  • couple waves of enemies, lots of screenshake
  • super elevator level represents the elevator level of a beat em up
    • enemies falling in from floor above this one
    • i have this idea of the boss’s office at the top, and it’s just full of enemies being pushed into this hole in the floor

flower eater and dot hop

[b-reel: flower eater and dot hop]

  • fediverse jam (summer 23)
  • I’d been curious about puzzlescript since I saw a talk featuring A Good Snowman Is Hard To Build
    • I thought it’d be more of a commandline tool
    • but, it’s pretty good for what it is, and I got a puzzle game going
  • Later I wrote a puzzle-script-like parser in gdscript
    • it’s not generic!
    • I reimplemented flower-eater’s logic in gdscript (with unit tests!)
    • renamed to dothop
  • Later added more puzzles
    • and i’m pulling it into it’s own repo!

[but what about monorepos!]

  • yeahhhhhh…… whatever…….
  • this puzzlescript influence was huge for dino
    • it has evolved into what i’m calling ’levelscript’ and dino’s ’brick’ addon
